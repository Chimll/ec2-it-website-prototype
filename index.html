Resources / FAQ

Common questions about managed IT support.

What does managed IT support include?

It usually includes helpdesk support, user and device administration, Microsoft 365 support, monitoring, patching oversight, supplier coordination and regular service review. The exact scope depends on the package.

How much does IT support cost for a London SME?

Costs usually depend on user count, systems, locations, required response levels, security needs and whether project or strategic work is included. See the pricing guide for indicative ranges.

What is included in your packages and what is extra?

Core support and management are packaged clearly. Hardware, software licences, major projects, complex remediation and out-of-hours work are normally scoped separately.

How does onboarding work?

Onboarding typically starts with discovery, access and documentation, followed by checks on users, devices, Microsoft 365, backup, security controls and support processes.

Do you work with internal IT teams?

Yes. EC2 IT can work as the sole IT provider or alongside an internal IT person who needs additional capacity, escalation support or project input.

Do we need 24x7 support?

Many professional SMEs do not need full 24x7 support. The right answer depends on working hours, client commitments, system criticality and risk appetite.
