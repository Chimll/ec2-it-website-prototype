FAQ

Questions this section can answer over time.

Should a small business have a managed IT support contract?

It depends on how much the business relies on its systems, how quickly users need help and whether there is enough internal capacity to manage security, backup and suppliers.

What information should we gather before speaking to an MSP?

User count, locations, key systems, Microsoft 365 setup, current suppliers, known issues, backup approach and any upcoming projects are useful starting points.

Can practical IT improvements be made before changing provider?

Often, yes. Documentation, support routes, user permissions, backup checks and Microsoft 365 settings can usually be reviewed before a larger supplier decision.
