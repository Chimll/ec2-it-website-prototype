Who we help

A practical fit guide for professional SMEs considering managed IT support.

EC2 IT is best suited to London and City-based businesses that need responsive day-to-day support, clearer IT management and a supplier who can work with commercial priorities.

Best-fit clients

  • Professional services and advisory firms
  • Commercial SMEs up to roughly 200 staff
  • Firms where IT affects client service or operational continuity
  • Businesses that want clear scope and accountable support
  • London professional SMEs with Microsoft 365 and cloud systems

Common situations

  • Current support arrangements are hard to assess
  • Users need a more responsive support route
  • Microsoft 365 has grown without much structure
  • Security and backup need practical review
  • Internal IT needs capacity, cover or escalation

Not ideal fits

EC2 IT may not be the right fit where a business only wants occasional break-fix support, has no appetite for basic security and documentation, or needs a very large enterprise outsourcing model.

  • One-off consumer-style support
  • Procurement based only on the lowest monthly price
  • No willingness to document systems and responsibilities
  • Highly specialised enterprise support needs outside SME scope

Outsourced IT vs co-managed IT

Outsourced IT means EC2 IT acts as the main support provider for users, devices, systems and suppliers. Co-managed IT means EC2 IT works alongside an internal IT person or operations lead to add capacity, structure and escalation support.
